## 1. WHAT IS MOGOVERSE

Mogoverse is the self-serve, AI-powered sonic branding platform by BrandMusiq. It democratizes what agencies charge $15K–$500K for — creating and managing brand audio identities — using AI trained on BrandMusiq's 10+ year methodology (MUSE framework, 40+ brand portfolio including Mastercard, Zomato, HDFC Bank).

**Core product capabilities:**
- AI sonic logo / MOGO® creation
- Brand audio asset management
- Search by mood, emotion, BPM, persona
- AI jingle/background music generation (brand-aligned)
- Mini-MOGO® (notification/app sounds)
