Sonic branding is the strategic use of sound to reinforce brand identity across every customer touchpoint. It includes sonic logos (short audio signatures like Intel's bong or Netflix's ta-dum), brand music, notification sounds, and audio guidelines. A MOGO (Musical Logo) is a short audio signature — typically 3 seconds — that captures a brand's essence in sound. Coined and trademarked by BrandMusiq, a MOGO is the audio equivalent of a visual logo. It sits within a three-tier sonic identity system: the MOGOSCAPE (full sonic landscape), the MOGO (sonic logo), and the Mini-MOGO (compressed version for notifications and app sounds). Mastercard's MOGO, created by BrandMusiq, is heard 500M+ times per year across 57 million merchant outlets.

MUSE (Musical Strategy Exercise) is BrandMusiq's proprietary methodology for creating sonic identities. It starts with a brand discovery workshop that maps two dimensions: Avatar (brand personality across 4 archetypes: Stability, Belonging, Mastery, Independence) and Rasa (brand emotion using the Indian classical Nava Rasa system of 9 emotions). This mapping generates "sonic moodboards" that guide the creation of the MOGO and MOGOSCAPE. Mogoverse brings MUSE to AI — the same framework that created Mastercard's 5x award-winning sonic identity, now accessible to any brand.
